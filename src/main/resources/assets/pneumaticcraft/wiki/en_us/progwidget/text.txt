[prefix{l}]The 'text' (Text) piece [prefix{}]
This piece can only be used as parameter for other pieces. It can be used in the following places:

For supplying an [link{pneumaticcraft:misc/entity_filter}]entity filter[link{}] to:

• [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/entity_attack}]Entity Attack [link{}] pieces.
• [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/entity_right_click}]Right Click Entity [link{}] pieces.
• [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/entity_import}]Entity Import [link{}] pieces.
• [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/condition_entity}]Condition: Entity [link{}] pieces.
• [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/drone_condition_entity}]Drone Condition: Entity [link{}] pieces.

For supplying textual data to:

• [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/edit_sign}]Edit Sign [link{}] pieces.
• [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/rename_drone}]Rename Drone [link{}] pieces.
• [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/jump}]Jump [link{}] and [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/label}]Label [link{}] pieces.
• [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/wait}]Wait [link{}] pieces.
• [link{pneumaticcraft:progwidget/conditions}]Condition [link{}] pieces.
